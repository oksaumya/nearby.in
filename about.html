About Us

Our Story

Founded in 2023, Nearby Services was born out of a simple yet powerful idea: to make finding reliable local service providers as easy as a few clicks. We understand the challenges of finding trustworthy professionals for your everyday needs, and we're here to change that.

Our platform connects service seekers with verified local professionals, ensuring quality, reliability, and peace of mind. Whether you need a tutor for your child, a plumber for a leaky faucet, or help around the house, we've got you covered.

We believe in empowering local service providers by giving them the tools and visibility they need to grow their businesses while providing customers with a seamless experience.
